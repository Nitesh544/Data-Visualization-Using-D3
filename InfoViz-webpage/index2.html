World’s Top 2% Scientists Dataset Visualization using D3

The visualization showcases a Zoomable circle packing of Top Scientists across the globe in each Country


Data Set: The dataset comprises a public database of highly cited scientists, providing standardized information on citations, h-index, co-authorship adjusted indexes, and a composite indicator (c-score). It differentiates between career-long and single recent year impacts, offering metrics with and without self-citations. Scientists are categorized into 22 scientific fields and 176 sub-fields, with percentiles available for those with at least 5 papers. The data is based on Scopus, updated to the end of the citation year 2021, including 195,605 scientists for the career-long database and 200,409 for the recent year dataset.
